## How to use this file

The psychosis and personality-disorder / complex-presentation layer — both **clinician-led**.
What's useful for self-help here is mostly a set of *cautions*: with these presentations,
standard imagery and personified compassion work can flood or destabilize, so the bar for
what you run is much lower and safeness comes first. Read `safety.md`. Assumes `model.md` and
`core-cmt.md`.

## Psychosis (CFTp) — clinician-led

The defining adaptation: **CFTp does not begin with compassion work — it begins with
establishing safeness**, the felt sense of the relationship as non-threatening, and only
introduces compassion after that's genuinely established (often over many sessions). Distress­
ing voices are worked with as *relational entities* — related to compassionately rather than
eliminated — and unusual experiences are normalized via the evolutionary frame. All of this
needs a CFT-trained clinician. In self-help: **do not use personified imagery** (compassionate
other, compassionate self as a figure) and do not attempt voice work. Offer only the lowest-
risk grounding and the de-shaming/normalizing framing, and signpost.

## Personality / complex presentations

For long-standing shame-based identities with very high threat reactivity, the documented
barriers to imagery are real: weak imagery ability, strong fears/blocks/resistances to
compassion, little compassionate experience to draw on, dissociation, the belief that
compassion is weakness, and shame-based self-identity. The clinical approach is **slower CMT
pacing, explicit FBR work** (clinician-only, `advanced-clinician.md`), and the
**Compassionate Kitbag** (`core-cmt.md` 10) — physical sensory objects as anchors — *before*
imagery is attempted at all.

## The self-help-safe layer only

1. **Soothing Rhythm Breathing** (`core-cmt.md` 1) and basic grounding.
2. **Compassionate Kitbag** (`core-cmt.md` 10) — the body-first anchor for when imagery
   floods; often the right and only rung here.
3. **De-shaming / normalizing psychoeducation** (`model.md`), paced very gently.
4. Imagery (safe place, and especially any personified figure) only with extreme caution and
   not at all where psychosis or dissociation is present — that's clinician work.

## If it stalls

- Imagery floods or destabilizes → stop; ground; SRB; the kitbag; do not push imagery.
- Voices, unusual experiences, or dissociation are central → clinician-led CFTp; offer
  safeness-first grounding and signpost (`safety.md`).
- Very high threat reactivity blocks everything → slow right down; the kitbag and SRB may be
  the whole of the safe work; name FBR/imagery work as goals for therapy (`advanced-clinician.md`).
