## How to use this file

The chronic-pain and physical-illness layer. CFT's angle: persistent pain and illness keep
the **threat system** chronically activated (the body itself becomes the threat), often with
a layer of **shame** about being unwell, dependent, or "not coping". The soothing system is
the counterweight. Set realistic expectations — compassion work helps mood, the shame, and
the relationship to the body far more than it changes raw pain intensity. Assumes `model.md`
and `core-cmt.md`. Deliver per `walkthrough-protocol.md`.

## The frame: illness as a threat-system activator

Get this across early and warmly: the distress around pain or illness isn't weakness or
over-reaction — a body in pain or under threat *is* a threat signal, and the system is doing
its job. Add the shame layer if it's present ("of course it's hard to need help — and needing
help isn't a failing"). This de-shaming (`model.md` 1-2) is itself part of the intervention.

## The sequence

Above the shared baseline (psychoeducation + SRB):
1. **SRB** (`core-cmt.md` 1) — down-regulating a threat system the body keeps re-triggering.
2. **Safe / Soothing Place** (`core-cmt.md` 2) — a soothing-system counterweight that asks
   little of a depleted, hurting person.
3. **Three circles, illness-framed** (`model.md` 1) — naming illness as a threat-system
   activator and the soothing system as what we're growing.
4. **Compassionate Self** (`core-cmt.md` 5) — meeting the pain, the limits, and the self-
   criticism about being unwell with warmth and the courage to keep living by what matters.

## If it stalls

- The user expects the pain itself to drop → re-set expectations kindly: the aim is the
  relationship to the body, mood, and the shame, plus more room to live — not making the pain
  vanish.
- Too depleted or in too much pain for imagery → SRB and the **Compassionate Kitbag**
  (`core-cmt.md` 10) as a body anchor; keep it small.
- Shame about being unwell is the main weight → work it as shame (`shame-self-criticism.md`).
- Backdraft when warmth enters → normalize, step down to SRB, compassion-to-others first
  (`core-cmt.md` 6, `model.md` 4).
